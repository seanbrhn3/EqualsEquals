Brand

Being engineering and computer science students ourselves we were all familiar with the notion of a weekend spent on our computers, laughing and joking with our peers, while simultaneously doing what we all love. Building...and creating. But this one would be different, it would be a celebration of diversity, a jamboree of inclusiveness

an entire weekend dedicated to fostering an environment in which minorities would feel empowered to harness their creative, technical, and innovative skills to produce something meaningful...and thus, Equals Equals was born.
